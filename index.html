Why

We want to end news droughts — and bring more of the world to the people the feed leaves out.

Local news is thinning, and the feed keeps narrowing what each of us sees. So we stand up small, local circles of coverage — in different languages, for different communities — to put more of the story in front of more people. More news, where there's too little.
